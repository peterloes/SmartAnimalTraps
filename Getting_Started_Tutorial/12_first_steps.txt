Servomotor electronics

Logic module is wired to S2.
Radiomodule is wired to D1_CMOS.

Circuit board: https://github.com/peterloes/SmartAnimalTraps/blob/main/Schematics/radio_module_V1.0.sch.pdf


Shutter will be at the beginning always closed!

1. Programming servomotor position shutter open
2. Programming servomotor position shutter closed
3. Shutter speed

- Reconnection the Servo electronic-board -- > Shutter should move to closed.
- Press hand-held transmitter button 2 for open the shutter.


hand-held transmitter

- Button 1 is to close the shutter.
- Button 2 is to open the shutter.
